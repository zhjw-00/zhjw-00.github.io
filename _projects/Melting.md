After selecting an iteration, the prototype is further developed by capturing and reprojecting its profiles from different viewpoints. The 3D model is then flattened into a 2D pattern, with different layers used to represent depth and graphic division through interlocking, overlapping, and etching.

The 2D pattern is converted back into a 3D model, and various fabrication methods (robotic drawings, thermal forming, laser cutting, 3D printing, etc.) are applied to construct the installation.
